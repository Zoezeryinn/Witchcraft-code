Ease elixir
The Ease Elixir works immediately, easing tension and calming racing thoughts.
The drinker feels enveloped in a warm, serene aura, floating on a gentle cloud under a starry sky.

To achieve the desired effect chant the spell:

"Ease my mind, gentle as dew,
A tranquil heart, steady and true.
By stars above and roots below,
Grant me peace; let it flow."

Flicker of Resolve
one - Get a clear quartz crystal, a white candle, a sprig of rosemary, a pinch of cinnamon, parchment paper, a pen, a fire-safe bowl, and a bowl of water.

two - Light the candle to cleanse your space, hold the crystal, and set your intention by saying,

“I radiate confidence and attract positivity in all that I do.”

three - Write your affirmation on the parchment.

four - Place the rosemary and cinnamon in the fire-safe bowl, then burn the parchment with the candle flame, letting the ashes fall into the bowl.

five - Repeat,
“As this flame transforms, so too do I.”
six - Put out the fire with water, hold the crystal to your heart
seven - ⁠Thank the candle and dispose of the remains respectfully.

Abundance
The Abundance Charm conjures a feast from thin air, filled with delicious, perfectly prepared dishes to satisfy every craving.
As the spell is cast, the scent of fresh-baked goods and ripe fruits fills the space.

To summon the feast, recite:
"From air to plate, my hunger wanes,
A feast appears where none remains.
By hearth and home, by fire and art,
Bring forth the meals to fill my heart."
The magic is fleeting; the feast lingers only for an hour before fading like morning mist. Use it in times of true need or celebration.

Clarity
Light four red candles, one for each direction of the cardinal directions.
Speak an intention to each candle. the south is your past, something to let go of.
The north is your carrier forwards, something to look towards. The east and the west are your sisters, something to hold on to on your journey.
Use this ritual when desiring clarity, making difficult choices or gaining a sense of orientation in your life
